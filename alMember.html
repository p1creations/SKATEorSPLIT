Age 31, started skateboarding when I was 14 years old, grew up in Marion, IN. I had a strong passion for baseball until I discovered skateboarding!

Ever since then, I have never looked back. I enjoy skateboarding, hanging out with friends and having a good time.

I've had one previous sponsor - Bad Moon Skateboards, I am a member of the Marion Skateboard Advocates. I want to help others be able to experience the joy

I received throughout my many years of trials and tribulations on a skateboard.
