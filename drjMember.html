Daniel Jones

Brand Ambassador/Flow Team


Daniel is an Indianapolis, IN local. He's been skating since he was 13-years-old, and regularly practices a multitude of skate disciplines including street, freestyle, longboarding, slalom, and park.

In the past, he’s been sponsored by Gravity Skateboards, Envision Skate Collective, Full Armor Clothing, and Ayres Clothing. Currently, he rides exclusively for SKATE or SPLIT.

Past accomplishments include winning first place at the Longboard Freestyle contest at the Epic International Skate Fest in 2009, first place in Hybrid Slalom (C-Group) at the 2011 Texas Cold Fusion Sizzler, and second place in Tight Slalom (C-Group) at the 2011 Texas Cold Fusion Sizzler.

You can check out Daniel’s skating on Instagram, where his handle is @searching4animalchin
